About the study

Eligibility

Important requirement

This study is for newly approved medical cannabis patients who have not yet started taking medical cannabis. If you have already started your medical cannabis prescription or have been using medical cannabis for awhile, you are not eligible.

It is OK if you have purchased your prescription as long as you have not taken any yet.

Frequently asked questions

Will I ever receive any feedback or results from the DNA study? If no, why not?

No, unfortunately that is not possible. First, to protect your confidentiality, your samples will de-identified through the use of a meaningless ID code. This important privacy measure makes it impossible to provide individual results by matching samples with findings. Secondly, our Humans Subjects Committee forbids us from providing such feedback, in part because we do not know ahead of time what we will find, what significance it will have, or how people will deal with it.

What will the blood eventually be used for?

Genes are essentially instructions that your body uses to build proteins that carry out various functions in your body. Some proteins affect how the brain works. Others affect how well you can metabolise drugs, such as medical cannabis.
